Does this person need a RFAP?

You will be asked to provide details on whether:

  • the person has been living in an incentivised substance free living (ISFL) area in custody
  • the person has engaged with the DART team in custody
  • the person is willing to comply with the RFAP regime

These are not requirements for a placement in a RFAP. But the information will be used to help place someone in a suitable AP.
