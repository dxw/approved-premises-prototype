Apply for an Approved Premises (AP) placement

Use this service to apply for a placement in an Approved Premises (AP) on behalf of a person who'll be on probation.

If a person is leaving prison, you should aim to complete your application 6 months before they are released.

You can leave and return to your application at any time. Your progress will be saved.

Before you start

You need

  • an up to date OASys – we'll use this to pre-fill some of your application
  • the person's CRN
  • the oral hearing date, if applicable and if known

You need to know:

  • eligibility requirements - you'll need to demonstrate why an AP is necessary over other interventions and accommodation options
  • factors that will have an impact on the location, type of AP, and room required
  • how you'll prepare for move on after the AP
