This application is not suitable for an Approved Premises.

Based on the information you have provided, this person does not meet the requirements for placement in an Approved Premises.

Placements in AP are limited and only a small number of people are eligible.

To be considered for placement, the person must meet at least 1 of the following requirements:

  • categorised as tier A1-B3
  • serving a life sentence and in custody for 10 years or more
  • known to the public for committing a sex offence
  • recommended by MAPPA for an AP placement

The following must also be true:

  • an AP will enhance the individual's risk management further than any other licence conditions available
  • specific monitoring and control measures provided in an AP is recommended
